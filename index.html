Project Idea

The idea I have come up with is a workout app mainly targeting professional Athletes but accessible to everyone. The workout app will still have the basic features of any other workout app, planning workouts and tracking progress but the main difference will be data collection. After receiving permission to track the users workout and dieting progression, the data from the user will be collected and processed using machine learning that will creat personlised workout programs for someones spercific goals. A personal trainer in your pocket.

Motivation For building the app

My motivation behind this comes from my enjoyment of sport and the competitive nature of it. Many athletes and people are looking for effective ways to improve their physical performance and this app will provide taylor made advise and information that best suits that individual. In 2017 21.5% of the population fifteen years and older participated in sports related activities three or more times per week that’s 4.3 million people who could use the app to better their performance. It can be hard to compete in sport competitively and have the research and know how to best set up a program for the best individual results and this app idea will hopefully provide that.

What is the app

The app will be a simple to use fitness app that allows the user to track their fitness goals. Included in it will be a basic workout tracking section that allows you to input workouts, for example if you are bench pressing you could enter the amount of wait you are benching, the number of repartitions and the number of sets and it will keep track of this data so you can look back on results. There will also be a diet section you can track your protein, calories, fats and other nutritional factors. The app will use real world proven statistics and compiled data from all users of the app, and it will provide reasonable recommendation to the user to make changes to their workouts and diet. For example If someone was training to improve the max weight they can bench press, the app will use the information from others who have successfully increased their bench press and apply that info to users who are trying to increas there own bench press results

The first version of the app might be verry basic only providing advice on weight loss and how to improve on certain workouts most likely the three main compound movements people do, deadlift, bench and squat. Focusing on weight loss and these three compound movements allows time for the app to collect initial data but still provide productive information to users because almost every high-level fitness program consists of these exercises and a good diet. After the app has been running and has collected sufficient data it will be able to make educated guesses on what workout routines and what diets best suits individual users. The result is an app that allows you to track your fitness goals and provide advice to help you effectively and efficiently execute your goals.

The app will be powered by artificial intelligence that is focused on the users fitness goals. The app will with minimal help from people create its own fitness programs. This will eliminate the cost that is associated with personal trainers but still provide solid reliable advice

Tools to build application

The app will run on most smart phones so it will need a multi-platform front end coding language such as flutter. The apps back end needs to be a solid programming language that can handle data analytics and machine learning, python probably being the best option. The app will need to be developed on a PC or Mac and the information gathered will have to be stored on a server.
